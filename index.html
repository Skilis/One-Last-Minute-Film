One Last Minute

One Last Minute | A Tale of the Dead (2018)

R | 10min | Drama, Horror, Thriller | 2018 (USA)

Synopsis

Civilization has collapsed due to swarms of zombies overwhelming social, law-enforcement, and military structures. A small group of survivors must battle the dead, and ultimately each other, to remain among the living.

Cast and Crew

Directors: Andrew Hass, Kynda Laufmann
Writers: Andrew Hass, Kynda Laufmann
Starring: Mikayla Bordner, Seneca Smith, Edward Casto, and Aidan Smith
